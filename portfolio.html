What do I do?

Some of the things that I genuinely enjoy doing are retrocomputing and music compositions. I have been in these fields for as long as I have been interested in computers, and have collaborated with many people alongside in projects such as deep-theming Windows (in particular, theming 8.1 to look like a certain OS + customizing it further), installing DOS 6.22, Windows 95, 98, ME, 2000, and XP onto USB flash drives/hard drives on very modern hardware.

While these are niche "achievements," it certainly was among the best things that I've experienced within the tech world. Its retrocomputing aspect also shines in particular since computers from older generations need more manual control, which helps substantially with the level of engineering I yearn for.
